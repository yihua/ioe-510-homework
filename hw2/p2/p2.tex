\section{Exercise 2.2 Sparse solution for equations with {\tt AMPL}}
\textbf{Solution:}
To find the ``sparsest solution'' for a system $Ax=b$, we construct the 1-norm minimizing problem as follows,
\begin{equation}
\label{eq: sparse}
  \begin{array}{lrcll}
    \min
    & \multicolumn{4}{l}{\sum_{j=1}^{n} t_j} \\
    \text{s.t.}
    & t_j-x_j >=0, j=1,2,...,n\\
    & t_j+x_j >=0, j=1,2,...,n\\
    & Ax=b 
  \end{array}
\end{equation}
